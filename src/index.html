Why cycle-sync?

Did you know your needs change throughout your menstrual cycle? Hormone fluctuations can affect your energy, cravings, mood, and even how your body uses certain nutrients.

Moone adapts to each phase of your cycle, providing tailored recommendations for:

  • Menstrual Phase – Replenish iron and rest.
  • Follicular Phase – Boost energy with fresh foods.
  • Ovulatory Phase – Support hormones with healthy fats.
  • Luteal Phase – Satisfy cravings and balance mood.
